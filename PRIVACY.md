# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 24 July 2026_

TrueTime is a desktop stopwatch application. It is designed to work entirely
on your own device.

## What we collect

Nothing. TrueTime does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
information or usage data.

- **No accounts.** There is no sign-in, and no personal details are ever requested.
- **No analytics or tracking.** The app contains no telemetry, advertising, or
  third-party tracking of any kind.
- **No network connections.** TrueTime does not send your data anywhere. All
  functionality runs locally.

## Where your data lives

Your stopwatches and settings are stored **locally on your device**, in the
application's local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you
choose to export it.

- **Export/Import.** When you export a backup, the file is written only to the
  location you select, and is never uploaded anywhere. Importing reads only the
  file you choose.

## Windows features

If you enable Windows features such as OneDrive backup of app data, or install
the app to removable storage, that data is handled by Windows under Microsoft's
own privacy terms — TrueTime itself performs no such transfers.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this same
location with a new date above.
